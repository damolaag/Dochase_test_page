Rich Media Ads

With Rich Media Ads, you can capture the attention of potential and existing customers by prompting them to interact with the ad and provide a more engaging experience.

Rich Media ads present new experience and measurable statistics including interactions with sections of the banners and Banner purchases. This takes away ads from irritation to fascination.

Rich media advertising format is all about flexibility - it floats, plays, and expands while the user interacts with a landing page.
